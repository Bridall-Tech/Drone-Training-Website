UMaT RPAS TRAINING

DRONE TRAINING SPECIALISTS

The philosophy and mission of UMaT RPAS Training is to develop world class RPAS/Drone pilots in a rapidly emerging industry and ensure our graduates
are ready to tackle problems and conditions in this complex field.

Personnel trained from UMaT RPAS Training to use RPASs, will use it efficiently to provide professional services such as monitoring illegal mining activities,
concession boundaries, compliance, stockpile and volume computations, providing surveillance for forests and water bodies, videography, photography, exploration and other related professional work.
