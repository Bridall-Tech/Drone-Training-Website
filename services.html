ABOUT RPAS TRAINING

The UMaT Remote Pilot Aircraft System (UMaT RPAS) training aims to produce competent and knowledgeable RPAS/Drone
Pilots to respond efficiently and use drone technology to monitor various activities effectively and efficiently while applying RPAS rules and regulation as
stipulated by GCAA and able to fly safely.

BASIC REQUIREMENT

No prior experience required. The course is specially targeted at all professionals, researchers and environmental
consultants that could benefit from the use of Drone technology. Participants MUST be 18 years or above
and medically fit, NOT visually impaired and MUST also be able to read and write.

OUR MODULES

Introduction to RPAS Systems

RPAS Regulations

Human Factors

Weight and Balance

Construction and Parts

Flight Plans

Principles of Photogrammetry

Introduction to GIS

Flight simulation of RPAS

Manual and Autonomous RPAS Flight practice

Image Processing and Analysis
