OUR TEAM

Our RPAS instructors are responsible for training participants to effectively and safely pilot an RPAS (Drone) according to the
Ghana Civil Aviation Authority rules and regulations. Our instructors take individual participants through all modules and guide them through their fieldwork sessions.
They also pay closer attention to participants who are new to the concept of RPAS. They examine individual participants and prepare them for GCAA
knowledge and Skills Test. Our instructors are disciplined, skilled and hardworking. Some duties of our instructors include but not limited to training participants to:

1. Train drone pilots to adhere to GCAA regulations for safe flight .

2. Interprete weather apps to forecasts to ensure safe flights.

4. Document pre/ post-flight and simulation log sheets.

5. Plan drone flight routes and gather information or produce aerial photographs.

6. Perform testing troubleshooting, and maintenance on equipment.

7. Process and comprehend the information obtained.
